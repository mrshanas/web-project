About Vecna Secondary School

Vecna Secondary School was established in 1918 by his honorable Prof. Shanas Von Vecna. Since then the school has had more than 20000 graduates from it joining high schools. This school was established mainly to help special group students who are struggling with society oppression.
The school is based for o-level education with the Cambridge and IB curicullum.
The eligiblity of students is based on merits and sports talents and the status of needy of a student.
The school ahs a total of 57 staff members 23 of which are intellects and the rest non-intellects. According to the student magazine the most stated hard subject is Mathematics for Engineers.

Vision

Having the most tallented and innnovative society through educating the youth.

Mission

Providing special grouped youths with a better and deserved eduavtion.
